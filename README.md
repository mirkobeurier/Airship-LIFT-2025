# Airship-LIFT-2025
Design of the sytems involved in controlling, flying and operating a scaled airship. 

## Overiview
Co-organized by FLYING WHALES and Planète Sciences, this annual competition invites teams of engineering students to take on an exciting challenge: to design an airship capable of carrying loads, starting from a blank envelope, essentially, a mini version of the LCA60T!
This is airship was built on the base of the prevos edition airship, with clear objectives to have a better chance at completing different challenges and come on top

## Objectives
- Redesign of the propulsion system to have better handling characteristics and better suited for the propulsion needs of the airship
- Redesign of the electrical distribution system with a custom made PCB
- Redesign of the grappler to improve reliability 

## Features
- 6 Meter length
- 3.5 kg mass combining all systems
- 6 vectorized electric motors
- Grappler and sky crane to transport objects

## Tools used
- 3D exp
- Fusion 360
- Easy EDA
- 3D printing
